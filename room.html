Gameplay:
  • create a room, then use the link to invite others
  • maximum of four players per room
  • rooms are cleared after five minutes of no players
Mouse Controls:
  • click a card to select it
  • click and drag or click + ctrl to select/unselect multiple cards
  • double click a card to flip it over
  • double click a corner to claim a private area
  • click and hold to move selected cards
  • right click and hold to view an enlarged version of a card
Keyboard Controls:
  • s = shuffle selected cards
  • d = form a deck with selected cards
  • f = flip over selected cards
